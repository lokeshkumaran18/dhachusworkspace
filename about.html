Welcome to Panimalar Engineering College

The mission of Panimalar Engineering College, a Christian Minority Higher Education Institution overseen by the JAISAKTHI Educational Trust, is to provide aspirant young with a top-notch engineering education. The College has received approval from the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi, and recognition from the UGC with statuses 12(B) and 2(f). The college is conveniently positioned close to Poonamallee in Chennai, with excellent road access to the districts of Thiruvallur, Kancheepuram, and Chennai. In 2000, the Trust established Panimalar Engineering College in compliance with the Tamilnadu government's general strategy. The policy places a strong emphasis on meeting the need for skilled engineers for a range of industrial and development initiatives in Tamilnadu and throughout the rest of India.
